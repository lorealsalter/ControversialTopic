Pros of Affirmative Action

    Reduces Discrimination

  • Affirmative action aims to address historical and systemic discrimination faced by marginalized groups.

    • Ensures Social Equity

  • Affirmative actions helps mitigate the disadvantages faced by marginalized communities and offers a chance for historically excluded people to access education and professional opportunities.

    • Enhances Social Mobility

  • Affirmative action can contribute to breaking the cycle of generational poverty and limited opportunities by providing access to educational and employment opportunities to people from disadvantaged backgrounds.
